Built for the People Doing the Work

This Is for the Small Contractors Carrying the Weight Every Day

Granite Models exists to help the tradesmen, owner-operators, small crews, and contractors who are out there every day doing the work, solving problems, taking care of customers, and trying to keep their businesses moving.

This platform is built from real field experience — not from a boardroom. After decades in the trades, we know what it feels like to come home worn out and still have estimates, paperwork, calls, scheduling, equipment issues, customer follow-ups, and business decisions waiting.

The goal is simple: build tools that save time, reduce stress, organize the business, and give contractors better systems to work with.

Steel Fabrication Spotlight · In Development

Building a Mini Operating System for the Shop

The steel fabrication system isn't finished yet — but a lot of the pieces are already built and working. The goal is one connected operating system for a fab shop: a shop dashboard for jobs, scheduling, and operations; a service ticket toolbox for maintenance and requests; a steel shape library; and a scope identification program — tied together with AI-assisted support.

The steel fabrication dashboard runs on a different level than a standard trade dashboard — more automated and more connected. The dashboard and each tool are separate paid products; the tools aren't bundled free with the dashboard. More pieces and demos are added as they're finished.
