What it does

Photo → route in 30 seconds

Snap a photo of a handwritten list or paste a block of addresses. Claude Vision OCR pulls the stops, geocodes them, and orders them for the shortest drive starting and ending at your shop.

Weekly schedule

Master mow list, Monday through Saturday day-bucketed. Same schedule repeats every week and resets on Monday. Per-stop completion checkboxes for the crew.

Weather-aware rescheduling

7-day forecast next to the schedule. When the day goes sideways — rain, equipment, short crew — one button balances the unfinished stops across the rest of the week.

Mobile PWA, install in 30 seconds

Add-to-home-screen install on iPhone and Android. Works on the truck, on the porch, between stops. Cached so it’s usable when the signal isn’t.

Who it’s for

Crew-based trades where the route is the day — lawn / landscape, paving, snow, irrigation, septic service, anything where a foreman drives a list of stops every morning.

Crews tap-and-go. The boss manages the master property list, adds notes (gate codes, special requests, what was done last time), and watches the week. No per-user fees — bring as many crew members as you need.

FAQ

Does it require GPS tracking on my crew?

No. Route Scheduler does routing, not surveillance. You tell it where the stops are; it tells the crew the order.

Can I use my own map?

In-app map uses Leaflet with OpenStreetMap tiles. Export to Google Maps with one tap (auto-splits when you have more than 9 stops, because Google Maps caps waypoints).

How many properties can I add?

Unlimited. Build your master mow list once; it repeats every week and resets on Monday.

Does it work offline?

The PWA caches the day’s schedule so a crew can keep working through a bad signal. Updates push to the server when the connection comes back.

Can I add it to my existing dashboard later?

Yes. The Route Scheduler data layer is designed to plug into a broader trade dashboard or the Granite Pro stack when you’re ready.

What happens if I cancel?

You keep access until the end of the period you already paid for. No refund. After that, your account goes inactive and the data stays archived for 90 days in case you come back.
