3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to provide and maintain our software services, process payments and manage subscriptions, communicate with you about your account, improve our platform and develop new features, comply with legal obligations, and protect the security of our systems.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. 12. QuickBooks Online Integration

Our dashboards offer an optional integration with Intuit's QuickBooks Online. This integration operates only after you explicitly connect your QuickBooks account using Intuit's secure authorization (OAuth 2.0). We never see or store your QuickBooks password.

What we access. With your authorization, we access your QuickBooks company name (to confirm the connection) and the customer and invoice records involved in a sync you initiate. We request only the accounting scope (com.intuit.quickbooks.accounting) required for this purpose.

How we use it. We create or update customers and invoices in your own QuickBooks company to match the records you choose to send from your dashboard. The direction and timing of every sync is controlled by you. We never sell QuickBooks data or use it for advertising or for any purpose other than performing the sync you request.

Where authorization tokens are stored. The access and refresh tokens that authorize the connection are stored only within your own dashboard instance. They are not shared with other customers and are not stored on any shared Granite Models server.

Disconnecting. You may disconnect QuickBooks at any time from your dashboard's Integrations page. Disconnecting revokes the tokens with Intuit and removes them from your dashboard. Your use of QuickBooks is also governed by Intuit's terms and privacy policy at intuit.com/privacy.
